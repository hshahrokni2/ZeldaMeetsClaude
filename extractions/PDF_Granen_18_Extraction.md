## POST-BALANCE SHEET EVENTS (Note 16)

The board continues to follow the 2015-2035 maintenance plan. Work on facades facing Valhallavägen and Villagatan continues during spring 2020.

**COVID-19 Related Postponements:**
- Planned pipe filming and flushing postponed due to infection risk
- Basement corridor painting postponed (pending pipe renovation analysis)

**Completed/Ongoing Projects (Early 2020):**
- Both elevators renovated in February 2020, including:
  - Technical renovation
  - Replacement of worn parts
  - Installation of photocells (new EU safety standard)

**Planned Projects:**
- Outer yard renovation facing Villagatan
  - Newly planted trees have taken root
  - Drainage needed along building walls to prevent basement moisture
  - Soil depleted and in need of new earth and improvements for plant material

**Infrastructure Improvements:**
- Investigating improved internet reception
- Exploring use of pre-installed fiber technology
  - Gathering information on fiber providers
  - Seeking best value for money

**Waste Management Improvements (March 2020):**
- Both waste rooms repainted
- Increased recycling through food waste collection for biogas
- Household waste reduced, collection reduced to 2x/week
- Scrapping waste chute and "waste carousel" (workplace safety issue)
- Transition to two 370-liter bins (similar to bulk waste room)
- Improved paper packaging/cardboard sorting with dedicated 370-liter bin
- Reduced newspaper recycling bin from 370L to 190L (decreased newspaper volume)
- Board encourages members to use "Källsortera" app from Suez
